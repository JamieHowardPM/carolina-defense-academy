Carolina Defense Academy is being organized as a nonprofit initiative that supports practical self defense and safety education for individuals, families, and community partners throughout North Carolina.

Our focus includes:

  • Scholarships and financial assistance for self defense and safety training
  • Community workshops for schools, organizations, and local groups
  • Partnerships with trusted training providers in the Triangle region
