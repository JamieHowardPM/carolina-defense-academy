Carolina Defense Academy

Community safety through education, access, and training.

Coming Soon

Carolina Defense Academy is being developed as a nonprofit-focused initiative to support practical self defense and safety education for individuals, families, and community partners across North Carolina.

The academy is intended to:

  • Support access to self defense and safety training for underserved communities
  • Offer educational workshops for schools, organizations, and local groups
  • Collaborate with trusted training providers in the Triangle region

What to Expect Here

As planning progresses, this site will expand to include:

  • Mission, values, and program overview
  • Information about leadership and governance
  • Details on community partnerships and training locations
  • Updates on program rollout and future opportunities to get involved
